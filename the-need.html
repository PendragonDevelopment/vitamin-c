The Need

We built Vitamin C to solve the real life frustrations of doctors and patients like you.

“After finishing my cancer care, I saw seven different types of doctors in three months. They could barely transfer my records to each other and I felt like my care was spiraling to nowhere.” – Patient Interview

“I care about my patients. I really do. I just can’t care for them the way I want to when my EMR system wont let me send them their labs or insert important lifestyle factors which are important for their care.”– Provider Interview

“…And that’s kind of how it was. I mean, I walked out of there the last day, and the doctor wiped his hands, pushed me off, and said, “All right, go back to college.” There was never really any thought of educating me or giving me support to figure out, how do I get back into my old life?” – Patient Chris Crichton interview from Livestrong.org

“One of the things that can get very confusing is all the medicines that they have to take and all the home care that you have to give. One of the things that helped us a lot was to make the schedules for all the medicines and find out about the side effects and all the things, what the medicines were for.” – Edgar Perez, caregiver for his eight year old son, interview from Livestrong.org
