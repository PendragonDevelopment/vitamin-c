The Vitamin

Vitamin C is a way for patients and providers to easily connect through flexible care plans. Best of all, Vitamin C is a true collaborative product, allowing developers, nonprofits, and governmental institutions the ability to build evidence based care plans that which patients can use.

What is Vitamin C?

Our patient platform makes it easy for your to keep your daily, monthly, and lifelong health goals in one place. Each step of the way earns you points which you can redeem for special perks. Vitamin C makes it easy for patients to then share and receive information on their progress from their providers quickly and securely.

Vitamin C also makes it easy for providers to manage their patient populations as individuals, with real time updates and information on patient milestones as they happen.
